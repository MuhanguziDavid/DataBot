Overview

DataBot is a Machine Learning-Enabled Data Analytics Service for Equity Trading Applications(ETAs). It is a middle-ware service that can be plugged into Equity trading applications to access the data generated by the application. This data may include; Market Capitalization, equity trading details, company portfolios,stock volumes, bid and ask prices,etc. DataBot automatically analyses and generates predictions basing on the pre-uploaded training set. The data generated from the ETAs is used to carry out predictive analysis.
This data is crunched using advanced techniques of machine learning and artificial intelligence to predict the behavior of a company. Predictive Analysis allows the traders to graduate from their monotonous tasks of order punching, to the more creative aspect of estimation, and then park their capital at the right places.
